Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept custom orders?

Yes! I love creating custom pieces. Please contact me with your ideas and I'll work with you to bring your vision to life. Custom orders typically take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity.

What's your shipping policy?

I ship within 2-3 business days of receiving your order. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. All items are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive safely.

Do you offer returns?

I want you to love your purchase! If you're not completely satisfied, please contact me within 14 days of receiving your order. I'll work with you to make it right.

Are your materials eco-friendly?

I'm committed to using sustainable materials whenever possible. This includes natural fibers, non-toxic glazes, and eco-friendly packaging.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, I ship within the United States and Canada. International shipping may be available for custom orders - please contact me to discuss.

How do I care for my handmade items?

Care instructions vary by item type. Each product comes with specific care instructions, and I'm always happy to provide guidance on maintaining your handmade treasures.
